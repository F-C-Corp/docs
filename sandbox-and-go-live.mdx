## Sandbox checklist

Before go-live, you should validate:

- operation creation, update, completion, and cancellation with `If-Match`
- split tender and partial approval completion payloads
- refund against prior sale
- offline queue replay
- export creation and ready notifications

## Production rollout guidance

Recommended sequence:

1. Validate the full contract in sandbox.
2. Enable `If-Match`, typed payments, and upstream references in production.
3. Dry-run exports and reconciliation.
4. Start live traffic with support monitoring in place.

## Go-live exit criteria

Do not go live until you can prove:

- every operation writer persists and reuses the latest `ETag`
- payment reporting is deterministic for retries
- support can trace requests by `request_id`
- your reconciliation job can process incremental exports
