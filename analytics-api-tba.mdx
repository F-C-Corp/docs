---
title: "Analytics API"
description: "Planned reporting endpoints for product, location, and POS performance."
---

The Analytics API will let you query aggregated insights from your POS operations without building your own reporting pipeline first.

You will be able to use it to understand how your business is performing across products, locations, registers, and time periods.

## What you will be able to do

- See which products and categories are most popular by revenue, quantity sold, and margin.
- Compare locations to understand which stores are performing well and which ones are underperforming.
- Track trends such as transaction volume, average basket size, refund rate, and payment mix.
- Break down performance by hour, day, location, register, cashier, or product group.
- Identify unusual patterns such as sudden drops in sales, rising refunds, or low-performing registers.

## Example use cases

- Find your top-selling items this week.
- See which locations are growing and which locations need attention.
- Understand peak hours so you can staff registers more effectively.
- Measure how discounts, refunds, and payment methods affect performance.
- Build dashboards for merchant reporting, regional comparisons, and operational monitoring.

<Info>
  The first release will focus on read-only analytics over POS operations, receipts, and register activity.
</Info>
