---
title: "Hosted Receipts API"
description: "Planned digital receipt pages and delivery workflows for legally compliant customer-facing receipt access."
---

The Hosted Receipts API will let you offer full digital receipts without building your own receipt hosting, rendering, and delivery layer.

Instead of rendering and serving receipts yourself, you will be able to create secure hosted receipt links that OpenFiskal manages for web, mobile, email, SMS, and QR-based receipt delivery.

The goal is to help you deliver legally compliant digital receipts in a format that is easy for customers to open, store, share, and use after the transaction.

## What you will be able to do

- Generate hosted receipt URLs for completed POS operations.
- Deliver digital receipts in a legally compliant format tied to the underlying fiscal record.
- Share receipts by email, SMS, QR code, or printed short link.
- Present receipts in a mobile-friendly web view with no app install required.
- Reuse the same hosted receipt across support, returns, loyalty, and post-purchase flows.
- Control branding such as logo, merchant identity, and contact details.

## Example use cases

- Show a QR code on the printed receipt so customers can open a digital version.
- Replace paper receipts entirely in checkout flows that support digital receipt delivery.
- Send receipt links by SMS after an EV charging session, kiosk purchase, or unattended sale.
- Let support teams retrieve and resend a hosted receipt without manually generating PDFs.
- Power self-service returns, warranty claims, or expense workflows using a stable receipt URL.
- Offer merchants a simple way to deliver paperless receipts across all locations.

## Planned features

- Expiring and non-expiring receipt links depending on the delivery flow.
- Branded hosted receipt pages per merchant or platform.
- Receipt views that include the legally relevant fiscal data, merchant details, and transaction summary.
- PDF download and print-friendly layouts.
- Optional delivery tracking such as sent, opened, and failed states.
- Search and retrieval by operation ID, receipt ID, or external reference.

<Info>
  The first release will focus on read-only hosted receipt rendering and delivery for completed operations, with an emphasis on digital receipt delivery and fiscal traceability.
</Info>
