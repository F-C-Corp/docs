Use the separate [Hosted Receipts API](/hosted-receipts-api) for receipt retrieval, PDF rendering, and delivery.

## Rendering rules

You must preserve fiscal fields exactly as returned by OpenFiskal. You can change layout, branding, and channel-specific wrapping only where the regime allows it.

### You must not change

- fiscal identifiers
- timestamp values
- totals and tax figures
- required merchant, location, and register labels
- regime-specific verification fields

### You may change when allowed

- typography
- paper width and line wrapping
- non-fiscal branding blocks
- localization of your own explanatory labels

## Hosted versus POS rendering

Use hosted rendering when you want OpenFiskal-managed PDF output and delivery workflows. Use POS rendering when you need immediate in-store print control, but only after you confirm that your renderer preserves the required fiscal content.

## Canonical flows

- Standard sale: complete the operation, then fetch or deliver the receipt
- Offline replay: issue a provisional receipt locally, replay the completion, then replace or attach the final hosted receipt
- Refund: create a refund operation linked to the prior sale and deliver the new receipt
